Lynqo Help & Support

Lynqo is a manual-first app for turning rough thoughts, errands, follow-ups, receipts, and reminders into one saved action fast. AI helpers are there when speaking, typing a rough note, or attaching a screenshot is faster than starting clean.

Getting Started

Use New Item when you already know what you want to save. Use Smart Capture or Voice + AI when speaking, typing rough notes, or attaching a screenshot is faster than starting from a blank form.

AI Helper Availability

AI helper features depend on Apple Intelligence support, a supported language, and the current readiness of required system models. If Lynqo shows that AI helpers are unavailable, you can still create and manage items manually.

Permissions

Voice features need microphone and speech recognition permission. Reminder alerts need notification permission. If permission is denied, Lynqo explains what is unavailable and can take you to the relevant iOS Settings screen when possible.

Saving and Editing

Lynqo is built around one-item creation. Review AI-filled content before saving it, especially titles, notes, amounts, and due dates. Saved items can be edited later from the timeline.

Reminders

Reminder items still save even if notifications are off. If alerts are important for your workflow, turn notifications on in Settings and verify the reminder date before leaving the item screen.
