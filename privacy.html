Lynqo Privacy Policy

Lynqo helps you turn rough thoughts, errands, follow-ups, receipts, and reminders into saved action items.

Last updated: July 2, 2026
Terms of Use Help & Support

Overview

Lynqo is designed around on-device storage and Apple-provided system features. This policy explains what Lynqo stores, how optional helper features work, and what choices you have while using the app.

Information Lynqo Stores

Lynqo stores the items you create, capture notes, reminder dates, and app preferences on your device. If you attach a screenshot, Lynqo stores that capture so the original input is preserved alongside the saved result.

Voice, Speech, and Screenshots

If you choose to use voice capture, Lynqo asks for microphone and speech recognition permission so it can turn your speech into editable text. If you add a screenshot, Lynqo may read text from the image to help fill one item. In the current version, raw audio is not kept after transcription finishes.

Notifications

If you turn on notifications, Lynqo can schedule local reminder alerts for saved reminder items. Reminder scheduling stays on your device and can be changed at any time in iOS Settings.

Data Sharing

The current Lynqo app does not include account creation, third-party ads, or analytics SDKs. The app uses Apple system frameworks for features such as speech recognition, notifications, and on-device AI helper readiness.

Your Choices

You can keep using manual item creation even if you do not grant voice or notification permissions. You can also review or remove saved items directly in the app.
